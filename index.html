Sivan Yonna

Opera and World Music singer- soprano

I graduated in Music at the Conservatory Niedermeyer and at the Ecole Normale de Musique in Paris .
I performed in various theaters of the Parisian region, Austria, Germany and in Italy as solist with chamber music and with orchestra. My different life experiences led me to collaborate with musicians of diffrent types of music from classical to world music. Currently I am part of the Duo Meitar as well as my solist career.
